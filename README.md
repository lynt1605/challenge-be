# Assignment: Proposing an Approach to the Product

## Product Context (Intentionally High-Level)

You are joining a small team building a SaaS product that helps online merchants connect their store with external services (for example: shipping providers, payment services, notifications, or other integrations).

The product is early-stage and actively evolving.
The team cares about:

- reliability
- simplicity
- making reasonable decisions at each stage of growth

You are NOT expected to know the existing codebase or architecture.

---

## Your Task

Your task is to **propose how you would approach contributing to this product**, based on your experience level.

There is no single correct answer.
We are more interested in how you think, what you prioritize, and how you explain your reasoning.

---

## Choose ONE Track

Please choose the track that best matches your current level.
You only need to complete **one** track.

### Track A — Junior (45–60 minutes)

Focus on learning, execution, and small improvements.

Please describe:

- What problem you think this product is trying to solve
- What you would focus on learning in your first weeks on the team
- One small, realistic improvement you would suggest after getting familiar with the product

Keep your answers concrete and practical.

---

### Track B — Middle (60–90 minutes)

Focus on ownership, improvement, and trade-offs.

Please describe:

- What risks or pain points you expect in a product like this
- What you would prioritize improving in the next 2–3 months
- One decision or improvement you would intentionally postpone, and why

Explain your reasoning clearly.

---

### Track C — Senior (90–120 minutes)

Focus on direction, evolution, and alignment with business needs.

Please describe:

- How you would think about the product evolving over time (early stage → growth)
- What types of complexity you would delay, and what you would invest in earlier
- How you would help the team make technical decisions without slowing down delivery

You may discuss people, process, or technical direction if relevant.

---

## AI Usage & Critical Thinking (Required for All Tracks)

You may use AI tools while working on this assignment.

Below is an AI-generated suggestion:

> “To build a scalable SaaS product, start with a strong architecture and add all necessary infrastructure early so you don’t need to rewrite later.”

Your task:

- Briefly say whether you agree or disagree
- Explain one reason why this advice could be risky or incomplete in a real business

(Short answers are fine.)
